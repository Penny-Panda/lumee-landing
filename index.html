How Lumee Works

Transforming your world into collectible cards is simple and fun.

1. Snap & Explore

Point your camera at anything - a favorite landmark, a curious pet, or a daily object. Lumee sees the magic hidden in plain sight.

2. AI Creates Magic

Our advanced AI instantly crafts a unique trading card, complete with captivating lore, balanced stats, and stunning artwork based on your photo.

3. Collect Your World

Build an ever-growing collection of cards that tell the story of your adventures and discoveries. Each card is a memory, a piece of your world.
