How Lumee Works

Whether you're learning or exploring, Lumee makes discovery simple and engaging.

1. Snap & Discover

Point your camera at anything - from everyday objects to famous landmarks. Lumee's AI instantly recognizes what you're looking at and prepares to share its secrets.

2. Learn & Create

Watch as Lumee transforms your discovery into an interactive card with age-appropriate content - from fun facts for kids to detailed information for travelers and collectors.

3. Collect & Compete

Build your collection, complete learning challenges, or engage in global turf wars. Every card you create adds to your journey of discovery and achievement.
